Stream UR MOM

My main goal is to create a fully functioning webpage for my friend’s artist collective, “UR MOM.” The two most important features of this website to me are that it must be functional on mobile devices and that it must be editable via a content management system. I will likely be utilizing WordPress for this, and I am considering starting with the “BizBoost” theme (https://wordpress.org/themes/bizboost). The webpage will consist of a homepage featuring photos of the artists, new releases, and information about the group as a whole. An interactive calendar showcasing upcoming important dates (new music releases, live shows, etc.) will either be displayed on this page or have its own separate link via the navigation bar. Said nav bar will also contain separate pages for each of the artists on the label:
• Niko Haley
• Loonieboys
• BAD DAY RODEO
• Roy Street
• CD Romance
Each artist’s page will contain a short biography, streaming and social media links, music videos, merch, and any other applicable materials.
